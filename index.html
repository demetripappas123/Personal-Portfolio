About Me

Hello, my name is Demetri Pappas, and I am a Junior at the University of Central Florida. I was first introduced to web application development through the Odin Project, an online course designed to take new learners from ground zero through a stack of technologies (css, html, react and Node.js specifically) to build competent developers.

Through the Odin Project, I developed a fundamental understanding of key technologies from the ground up, as well as a general idea of systems architecture. From there, I branched out into personal projects and freelancing, starting with a trading bot I am still working on, this portfolio, and a website commission for a financial company and a bridal store.

I intend to continue furthering both my skills in quantitative finance and computer science, integrating AI into my trading bot and exploring more, and further develop my reputation as a freelancer.
